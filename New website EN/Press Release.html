Green Horizons Launches Its Unique Balanced Speculative Account for Forex Brokerage Companies

In a new unique step in the region, GHVA is launching its unique Balanced Speculative Account (BSA) for use by forex brokerage companies to introduce to their clients.

The new BSA can be utilized by forex brokerage firms to broaden their client base by offering this product to clients with no prior experience in the markets, guiding them with pre-instructed formulas for trading to minimize their risk and maximize their returns.

The new BSA contributes to spreading investment knowledge and risk-return mechanisms among a wider base of clients, particularly youth such as college students and young graduates.

GHVA welcomes all interested brokerage firms to test the product and build a fruitful cooperation.
